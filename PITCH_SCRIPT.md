# Verdict — Pitch Script (~2:00)

**Format.** Open on Slide 1, click to Slide 2, then switch to the **live war room** for the run, and land on Slide 8 to close. The deck is bookends; the live demo is the hero. Target **1:50–2:10**.

**Before you start:** war room open at `http://localhost:8000`; the Terminal 3 sidecar running (`node notary_sidecar/server.js`); deck on Slide 1; pick a pre-staged company you've rehearsed (Sea is the cleanest).

> The moment that wins is the live **DENIED → AUTHORIZED** mandate gate. Slow down for it. Pause after it. Everything else is setup.

## The script

**[0:00 · SLIDE 1 — title]**
> "Investment due diligence takes an analyst **days**. And when they're done, you get a PDF that basically says — *trust me, I read everything.* You can't audit it. In a world of autonomous agents, an unauditable recommendation isn't a deliverable — it's a **liability**."

*(beat)*

> "This is **Verdict** — an AI analyst that earns a **verifiable mandate to act**."

**[0:18 · SLIDE 2 → switch to the WAR ROOM]**
> "Type a company, and a crew of five specialist agents researches it, reasons to a verdict — and **signs** it. Let me show you. Judge — pick one: **Sea, Grab, or GoTo**."

*(click the company, hit **Run**)*

**[0:30 · the run streams — narrate the spine, left to right]**
> "Watch the pipeline light up. **Scout** is pulling real, live headlines off the web with **Bright Data**, and finding the exact segment inside the earnings call with **VideoDB**. **Analyst** — that's **Kimi K2** — reasons over the evidence to a thesis and ranked risks. **Quant** runs the valuation in a real **Daytona** sandbox. **Producer** writes the memo, the deck, and the Excel model."

*(let it stream — don't rush; ~30s)*

**[1:05 · THE CLIMAX — slow down, point at the right panel]**
> "Now — the part that matters. **Notary** signs the report with the agent's Decentralized ID — real Ed25519. And here's what no other team can show you. Most agents treat identity as a *login badge*. Verdict makes it a **gate**."

*(as the gate fires)*

> "It requests a fifty-million-dollar position — **DENIED.** That exceeds its ten-million mandate. Then the in-scope action — commit the signed report — **AUTHORIZED.** And it lands, on-chain receipt and all."

*(hold 3 seconds on the green SEALED state — say nothing)*

> "Signed. Audited. In about ninety seconds."

**[1:35 · breadth + real — narrate over the war room]**
> "Underneath this run: **eight** sponsor platforms, each load-bearing — and five of them are **live right now**. Real web, real reasoning, real sandbox, real earnings-video search, real cryptographic signature. If any key or the network drops, that stage falls back to cache — so the run **always** reaches the signature."

**[1:52 · SLIDE 8 — close]**
> "Five stages. Eight platforms. One signed, auditable decision — in ninety seconds. Verdict doesn't just *read* the web — it reasons, values, and then **earns the right to act**, with a signature you can take to your board. Thank you."

## Delivery notes
- **The win is the DENIED → AUTHORIZED beat.** Slow down, gesture at it, and pause after. If you remember one thing, it's this.
- **Hands off the keyboard during the climax** — let the screen do the work.
- **Pre-staged pick = instant + unfakeable.** Power move *(only if you're confident)*: instead of the three chips, say *"actually — name any company"* and type a judge's suggestion. That triggers live Kimi + Bright Data research on anything (~15–20s) — higher risk, much higher reward.
- **If the run stalls:** keep talking; a global ~100s deadline forces it to the signature, and the credential still renders. Don't apologize, don't touch the keyboard.
- Map to the judges' rubric as you go: problem = *real-world value*, the gate = *innovation*, eight platforms = *breadth*, "live not staged" = *completeness*.

## Deck-only fallback (~90s, if you can't run live)
Walk Slides 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → **5 (read the DENIED → AUTHORIZED gate aloud)** → 6 → 8. Same words, minus the live narration. Have the recorded demo cued as a backup.
